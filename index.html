Lets Explore The Campus of The Woodford Reserve Bourbon Distillery!

The Woodford Reserve Bourbon Distillery was created in 1812 in Woodford County Kentucky. Before it became known as the Woodford Reserve Distillery, it was formely known as the Old Oscar Pepper Distillery and later the Labrot & Graham Distillery. It is about eight miles from the town of Versailles in north-central Kentucky, off U.S. Route 60 between Interstate 64 and Versailles. What's unique about this distillery is that it is very small distillery, yet produces one of the highest rated bourbons in the world and is now the main sponsor of the Kentucky Derby! The goal of this project is to create a few different maps to better understand the vegetaion of the campus, along with the topography of the area, and the campus's potential for solar PV installation!
